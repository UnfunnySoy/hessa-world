## Overview
Fauna is a classification of creatures that share animal attributes. This category sometimes overlaps with elements of the [[Deprecated/Monstra\|monstra]] classification, with creatures such as the [[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Spideer\|Spideer]] belonging to both categories due to their animalistic and monstrous features in conjunction. Detailed below are an amount of unique [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessan]] animals.

[[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Eastern Ice Perch\|Eastern Ice Perch]]: A large freshwater fish often caught for food.
[[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Frosilisk\|Frosilisk]]: A gargantuan snake with the ability to turn creatures into ice statues.
[[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Forgon Ice Clam\|Forgon Ice Clam]]: An abundant clam with valuable pearls.
[[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Glangoose\|Glangoose]]: A curious goose hunted and trapped throughout [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessa]].
[[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Hessan Great Cow\|Hessan Great Cow]]: A huge, hulking cow raised throughout [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessa]].
[[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Hessan Ice Wolf\|Hessan Ice Wolf]]: A large wolf with the ability to use minor magic.
[[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Mistbear\|Mistbear]]: A huge bear with the ability to conjure clouds of fog and teleport.
[[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Spideer\|Spideer]]: An elongated deer with many aspects of spider biology.
[[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Xanthous Crab\|Xanthous Crab]]: A yellowish crab that never stops growing under the right conditions.
