## Overview
[[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessa]] has a slew of unique and fantastic materials that populate the region.
[[Mundane Items/Materials/Adamantine\|Adamantine]]: A rare alloy created from adamantite, an ore found deep within the [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessan]] underground.
[[Mundane Items/Materials/Mithril\|Mithril]]: A strong, lightweight metal used in the production of various high-quality items.
[[Mundane Items/Materials/Throny\|Throny]]: An ivory-like substance that comes from the body of the [[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Hessan Great Cow\|Hessan Great Cow]].
