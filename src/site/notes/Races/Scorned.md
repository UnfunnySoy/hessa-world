## Overview
The Scorned are the result of the [[Altemair/Major History/The Spellsleet\|Spellsleet]] falling in [[Altemair/Altemair\|Altemair]]. Touched by the destructive power of the Spellsleet, each Scorned bear the marks of the cataclysm: strange concentric wrinkles, burns, and scars and purple, discolored skin.
## Mechanics
If your Human is Scorned, replace all Racial Traits found in the Player's Handbook with these, except for Age, Alignment, Size, and Speed.
***Ability Score Increase.*** When determining your character’s ability scores, increase one score by 2 and increase a different score by 1, or increase three different scores by 1.
***Languages.*** Your character can read, speak, and write Common.
