## Overview
## Mechanics
Snow Elf is a subrace of Elf.
***Ability Score Increase.*** Your Constitution score increases by 1.
***Snow Elf Weapon Training.*** You have proficiency with the spear, shortbow, net, and war pick.
***Cantrip.*** You know one cantrip of your choice from the Druid spell list. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for it.
***Wilderness Intuition.*** You are proficient in the Survival skill.
