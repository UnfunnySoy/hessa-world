## Overview
Undead are animated corpses raised through necromantic rituals and spells. A violation of the laws of nature and feared by civilization, undead carry fragments of memories of their past lives with them, unable to engage with the world the same ways they would have during life.
## Mechanics
***Ability Score Increase.*** Your Constitution score increases by 1.
***Age.*** Because an undead is the animated corpse of a once-living being, you not age. Functionally immortal, an undead persists until its body ceases to be suitable for animation, or once the effect maintaining its reanimation expires. You are immune to magical aging effects.
***Alignment.*** Undead are born of negative forces and are predisposed towards evil action, whether chaotic or orderly. Destruction, death, and violence are common strands shared by the reanimated, but rare undead who retain their memories of life have been known to act in ways that counter their evil nature.
***Creature Type.*** You are an Undead.
***Size.*** Undead vary in size depending on the size of their body before death. Your size is Small or Medium. You choose the size when you select this race.
***Speed.*** Your base walking speed is 30 feet. 
***Darkvision.*** You have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
***Undead Resistance.*** You are resistant to necrotic damage.
***Undead Restoration.*** When you would be dealt necrotic damage, you can use your reaction to absorb the negative energy, ignoring the damage and regaining a number of hitpoints equal to the amount of damage that would have been dealt to you instead. You may use your reaction in this way a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you complete a long rest. 
***Holy Susceptibility.*** You are vulnerable to radiant damage. When you take radiant damage, you cannot use your Undead Restoration feature until the start of your next turn.
***Undead Nature.*** As an undead, you do not need to concern yourself with the limitations of living creatures, represented by the following benefits:
- You have advantage on saving throws against being poisoned, and you have resistance to poison damage.
- You don’t need to eat, drink, or breathe.
- You are immune to disease.
- You don't need to sleep, and magic can't put you to sleep.
***Memories of Life.*** Fragments of memory from your previous life bleed into your undeath. You gain one tool proficiency and one weapon proficiency of your choice.
***Languages.*** You can speak, read, and write Common and one other language of your choice.
