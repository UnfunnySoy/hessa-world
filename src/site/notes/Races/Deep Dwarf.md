## Overview

## Mechanics
Deep Dwarf is a subrace of Dwarf.
***Ability Score Increase.*** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.
***Deep Dwarf Magic.*** You learn the mending cantrip. When you reach 3rd level, you can cast the *sanctuary* spell once with this trait and regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. When you reach 5th level, you can cast the *heat metal* spell once and regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. You can also cast these spells using spell slots you have of the appropriate level. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for these spells.
