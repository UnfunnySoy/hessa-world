## Overview
Adamantine is a rare metallic alloy produced from combining iron, gold, and a brittle metal called adamantite. In its strongest alloyed form, adamantine is a dark, crimson metal known for its legendary hardness and durability. When heated, adamantine becomes workable, though difficult and time consuming to forge.
