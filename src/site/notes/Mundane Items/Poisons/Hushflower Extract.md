## Overview
[[Hessa/Wildlife/Flora/Hushflower\|Hushflower]] petals, when soaked in oil or a strong alcohol for a few months, can create a thin, rosy fluid with a sickly vanilla smell. An individual who consumes an ample amount of the extract begins to feel the effects of the substance immediately, falling asleep outright or futilely attempting to maintain consciousness. The extract promptly loses potency after a victim passes out, meaning that an individual subject to the substance will continue to sleep for about an hour or until they are disturbed.
