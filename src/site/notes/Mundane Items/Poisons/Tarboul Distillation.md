## Overview
The boiled flesh of the [[Hessa/Wildlife/Flora/Tarboul Toadstool\|tarboul toadstool]] creates a thin, dark solution which can be reduced into a potent, dark fluid. The poison is somewhat delicate and is rendered inert by a small amount of a high-proof alcohol. The poison, once consumed by an individual, causes the  trachea to swell and close swiftly. This often leads to violent suffocation which kills the victim within a few minutes. If the victim is able to drink enough of a sufficiently strong spirit before their suffocation, the poison will be rendered inert and the victim will be able to breathe normally.
