## Overview
The [[Hessa/Wildlife/Flora/Shardstem\|shardstem]] plant creates a thick, milky sap when cut into. When enough of the sap is ingested, the effects of the poison quickly become apparent. An individual subjected to the poison becomes feverish and begins sweating profusely. A victim also often has minor hallucinations which often include visions of the dead, hearing voices from "beyond", and paranoia. These hallucinations paired with high body temperatures and constant sweating frequently lead to vomiting and difficulty completing simple tasks. The poison's effects typically only last for about a few short hours and rarely lead to death in a victim.
## Mechanics
*Poison (Ingested, 15gp)*
A thick, milky sap harvested from the shardstem plant and boiled into a viscous goo. It only produces its poisonous effects after greater than 2 ounces of the sap are ingested.
A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 1d4 poison damage and is poisoned for 1 hour. While poisoned in this way, a creature has vivid hallucinations of eerie and otherworldly subjects such as ghostly visions of deceased loved ones or ethereal renditions of music.
