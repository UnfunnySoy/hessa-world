## Overview
Alchemy is the practice of creating medicines, tinctures, salves, and potions from mundane, non-magical ingredients. Alchemy is practiced by few throughout [[Hessa\|Hessa]], as it requires access to numerous rare ingredients. As such, alchemy is practiced rarely outside of cities where consistent supply lines run.
