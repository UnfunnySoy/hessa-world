## Overview
In [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessa]], conflicts cannot always be resolved through legal representation and logical arrangements. As such, dueling is a common way to mediate disputes in areas the law cannot reach. Dueling in each province has different norms pertaining to how duels are arranged, what the stakes are socially and materialistically, and .
### [[Hessa/Regions/Forgon\|Forgon]]
Throughout Forgon, dueling is a common occurrence to manage disputes due to its low population and lack of formal law enforcement. 
### [[Hessa/Regions/Frostmar\|Frostmar]]
In Frostmar, dueling culture has largely died off; however, the symbols of early conflict resolution still resonate with many. Frostmar has few duels in its long history; however, each duel is definitively lethal.
In the early history of Frostmar, duels were arranged with the challenger or one of the challenger's closest associates approaching the challenged with a bundle of [[Hessa/Wildlife/Flora/Shardstem\|shardstem]] fibers and burning them.
