## Overview
The shardstem is a fibrous plant with hard, ice-like protrusions and growths. Shardstem bushes are not much more than five feet in diameter. The outer portions of the bushes and leaves take on a sky blue color while the inner flesh of the plant is a thin, snow-white fiber that expels a translucent, milky, sticky sap when cut. This plant is very resilient and can be found growing in the snow as far up north as [[Hessa/Civilization/Felmsgrace\|Felmsgrace]]. They are often used for making clothing, ropes, mats, walls, insulations, parchments, yarns, and more. Luxury canvas and tapestries made using high-end shardstem fibers are sold in auctions in cities to be used by professional artists and painters. The sap found in the plants is bitter and unappetizing, but is sometimes used in potions and teas to help increase the body’s ability to retain heat; however, too much sap can act as a [[Mundane Items/Poisons/Shardstem Sap\|poison]], making the consumer sickly and dehydrated while also giving mild hallucinations.
## Cultural Significance
The shardstem plant has extreme cultural significance throughout [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessa]]. The wide applications of shardstem fibers as well as the hallucinatory effects of the sap make the plant a symbol for many ideas, such as life, death, relationships, and spiritual connection.

Shardstem sap, due to its hallucinogenic capabilities, is a popular substance used by seers and mediums make contact with spirits, deities, and more.
