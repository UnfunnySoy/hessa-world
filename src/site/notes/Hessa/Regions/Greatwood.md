## Overview
The Greatwood is a [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessan]] [[Deprecated/Provinces\|province]] which constitutes most of the [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessan]] mainland. The Greatwood [[Deprecated/Provinces\|province]] reaches from the southwestern edge of the [[Cliffs of Canterra\|Cliffs of Canterra]] near the city of [[Hessa/Civilization/Thuves\|Thuves]] to [[Hessa/Civilization/Harbsend\|Harbsend]] south of the [[Fields of Leutece\|Fields of Leutece]] past the city of [[Hessa/Civilization/Helpherfrust\|Helpherfrust]] to the [[River Klein\|River Klein]].
