## Overview
Forgon is a [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessan]] [[Deprecated/Provinces\|province]] which begins in the wooded fields and bluffs north of [[Hessa/Civilization/Keppit\|Keppit]] and continues southwards past [[Lake Cerithene\|Lake Cerithene]] and through [[Garner's Woods\|Garner's Woods]] past the city of [[Wellstone\|Wellstone]].
