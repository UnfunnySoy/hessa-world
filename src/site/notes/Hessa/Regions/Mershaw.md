## Overview
Mershaw is a [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessan]] [[Deprecated/Provinces\|province]] which makes up a majority of [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessa]]'s southern tip. Mershaw stretches from just north of the city of [[Hessa/Civilization/Stomerstood\|Stomerstood]] across to just south of the city of [[Wellstone\|Wellstone]]. The [[Deprecated/Provinces\|province]] stretches southwards to the town of [[Markin\|Markin]] and across the southernmost coasts.
