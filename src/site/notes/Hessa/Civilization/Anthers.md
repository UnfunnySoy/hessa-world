## Overview
Anthers is a large, cattle-raising village north of [[Wellstone\|Wellstone]]. In Anthers, [[Races/Snow Elf\|snow elves]] and [[Races/Human\|humans]] live and work together, raising cattle and crops together. About 800 people live in Anthers, with travelers sometimes passing through as they travel northwards towards [[Hessa/Civilization/Keppit\|Keppit]] or southwards towards [[Wellstone\|Wellstone]]. Most working age persons in Anthers has some sort of agricultural job, spending much of their working hours tending to their fields, raising animals, or processing goods for sale in [[Wellstone\|Wellstone]] markets. Anthers, due to its low population compared to larger towns, is lacking in guards or other law enforcement. As such, most mediation and peacekeeping is done by the Barners, a wealthy [[Races/Snow Elf\|elf]] family that maintains a large farm and has made a minor fortune selling goods in city markets in [[Wellstone\|Wellstone]], [[Hessa/Civilization/Stomerstood\|Stomerstood]], and [[Markin\|Markin]]. 
## Resources
Of the resources gathered in Anthers, the most prevalent originate from the [[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Hessan Great Cow\|Hessan Great Cow]]. Most cattle farmers in Anthers make their livings through the butchery of the [[Hessa/Wildlife/Fauna/Hessan Great Cow\|cows]] and preparation of their meats, milk, fats, [[Mundane Items/Materials/Throny\|throny]], and hides. Other trade goods from Anthers include [[Hessa/Wildlife/Flora/Bellblock Bush\|bellblock berries]], [[Hessa/Wildlife/Flora/Inerga Root\|inerga roots]], wool and woolen products, and wheat flour all gathered from farms or foraged in the nearby area. 
## Climate
The climate in Anthers is cold most of the year, with temperatures not frequently exceeding 80°F in the summer and temperatures falling as low as -20°F in the winter. The village also receives large helpings of rain and snow due to its proximity to the nearby coast and [[Lake Cerithene\|Lake Cerithene]]. As such, inhabitants of Anthers share a large amount of experience in the maintenance of homes, windmills, barns, and such which are largely fashioned from cobblestone foundations and [[Hessa/Wildlife/Flora/Hessan Great Pine\|thick, waterproof planks of wood]].
