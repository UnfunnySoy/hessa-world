## Overview
Stomerstood is a primarily [[Races/Human\|human]] town near the southern point of [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessa]]. Here, [[Races/Human\|humans]] live tranquil, but hard-working lives. Common trades in Stomerstood include farming, fishing, logging, and mining. As Stomerstood contains a “younger” population, cultural norms pertaining to work and tradition are somewhat less strict. Change and its consequences are more common in Stomerstood; however, certain cultural norms pertaining to [[Hessa/Culture/Bremrest\|bremrest]], retirement, and a person’s role in society are held constant. Trade is very prominent in Stomerstood. With its close proximity to the [[Gulf of Freithel\|Gulf of Freithel]], the town often thrives off of its ability to build ships and conduct trade quickly and frequently. Stomerstood often gains many travelers through its city as people from outside the commonwealth and in the towns of [[Markin\|Markin]] and [[Wellstone\|Wellstone]] pass through en route to the more northern cities. Similarly, persons seeking to leave [[Hessa/Hessa\|Hessa]] often pass through Stomerstood and proceed by ship into more southern continents. The town itself is relatively large, resting at a comfortable 27,000 inhabitants.
