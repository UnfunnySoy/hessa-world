## Overview
## Mechanics
***Weapon (any sword), uncommon (requires attunement)***
A razor-thin edge with an iridescent mirror finish.
When you finish a long rest while attuned to Mirror Blade, roll three d20s and record the highest number shown. Until you complete a long rest, you may replace one attack roll made using Mirror Blade with this foretelling roll. You must choose to use the foretelling roll before the attack roll is made.
