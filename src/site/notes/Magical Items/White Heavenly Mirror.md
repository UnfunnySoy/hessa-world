## Overview
## Mechanics
***Weapon (dagger), very rare (requires attunement)***
A thin, razor sharp dagger that appears to be fashioned from a shard of a mirror.
You have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.
When you score a critical hit with this weapon against a creature, instead of dealing damage, you may instead entrap that creature within the blade. The blade can hold only one creature at a time. A creature trapped in the blade doesn't need to breathe, eat, or drink and doesn't age.
Additionally, you may use your action to trap a willing creature within 5 feet of you inside the blade.
While you are holding the blade, you can use an action to release the creature from the blade, putting the creature in the nearest unoccupied space to you. If you lose attunement to the dagger, any creature trapped within the blade is put into the nearest unoccupied space to the dagger.
