## Overview
## Mechanics
***Weapon (any sword), very rare (requires attunement)***
A weapon with twisting patterns of ice and hot iron.
You have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This weapon does either fire damage or cold damage instead of its normal damage types (you choose when you deal damage with this weapon).
Sword of Fire and Ice sheds bright light in a 10 foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet.
While you are wielding Sword of Fire and Ice, you are resistant to fire and cold damage.
