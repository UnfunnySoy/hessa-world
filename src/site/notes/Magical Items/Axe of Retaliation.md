## Overview
## Mechanics
***Greataxe, rare (requires attunement)***
A large, two-handed axe. The axe swings on its own accord when its owner’s blood coats the blade.
You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.
When you are dealt damage by an enemy within your reach, you may use your reaction to make a single melee weapon attack against that enemy using this weapon.
