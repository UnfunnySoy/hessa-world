## Overview
## Mechanics
***Armor (shield), rare***
A shield with a large sapphire worked into the center.
As a bonus action on your turn, you may attempt to shove against a single creature within 5 feet of you. Whenever you make a Strength (Athletics) check to shove a creature, you can treat a d20 roll of a 9 or lower as a 10.
