## Overview
## Mechanics
***Staff, rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster)***
A gnarled, wooden staff hewn from spruce. It smells faintly of chamomile. 
While you are attuned to Staff of the Sandman, you know the spell sleep and have it prepared (it does not count against the number of spells you can prepare). You may cast sleep without expending a spell slot once per long rest.
Additionally, when you cast sleep using a spell slot, you may increase the number of hitpoints the spell effects by a number of d8 equal to the level of the spell slot expended.
