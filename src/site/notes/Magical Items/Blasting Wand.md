## Overview
## Mechanics
***Wand, uncommon***
A thin length of wood with a small brass star fashioned onto the end.
This wand has 7 charges. While you are holding this wand, you may use your action to expend a charge and fire a crackling ray of flame towards a creature that you can see within 60 feet. That creature must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d4 + 4 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. 
The wand regains 1d6 + 1 charges daily at dawn. If you expend the wand’s last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the wand crumbles into ashes and is destroyed.
