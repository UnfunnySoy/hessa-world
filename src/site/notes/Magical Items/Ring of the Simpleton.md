## Overview
## Mechanics
***Ring, uncommon (requires attunement)***
A broad ring depicting a mask with a muscular arm sprouting from its mouth.
Your Intelligence score is 7 while you wear this ring if it is not already lower. Your Strength score becomes 19 if it is not already higher.
