## Overview
## Mechanics
***Weapon (any), rare (requires attunement)***
You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.
When you reduce a creature to 0 hitpoints using this weapon, you may use your reaction to move up to your speed without provoking opportunity attacks and make a single attack with this weapon.
***Curse*.** This weapon is cursed, and becoming attuned to it extends the curse to you. As long as you remain cursed, you are unwilling to part with the weapon, keeping it within reach at all times. You also have disadvantage on attack rolls with weapons other than this one, unless no foe is within 60 feet of you that you can see or hear. 
If you ever miss all of your attacks on your turn, you must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, you are stunned until the beginning of your next turn.
