## Overview
## Mechanics
***Weapon (any sword), rare (requires attunement)***
A thick crimson blade that is warm to the touch.
You may attune to or break attunement from this item as an action.
You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This weapon does fire damage instead of its normal damage types.
Embercleave sheds bright light in a 10 foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet.
You cannot be disarmed of Embercleave unless you are incapacitated. If Embercleave is on the same plane of existence as you, you can summon it as a bonus action on your turn, causing it to teleport instantly to your hand.
While you are wielding Embercleave, you may move through a creature’s space as if it were difficult terrain. You may not willingly end your turn in another creature’s space. If you end your turn in another creature’s space, you are pushed into the nearest unoccupied space, taking 5 force damage for every 5 feet moved.
