## Overview
## Mechanics
***Weapon (any sword), very rare (requires attunement)***
A blade crafted from glass. Half of the blade shows the wielder’s past, while the other half shows the wielder’s future.
You have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. 
When you reduce a creature to zero hit points with this weapon, you have advantage on all attack rolls you make with this weapon until the end of your next turn. Additionally, all attack rolls made against you are made with disadvantage until the end of your next turn.
