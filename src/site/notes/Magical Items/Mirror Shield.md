## Overview
## Mechanics
***Armor (shield), rare (requires attunement)***
A large, towering shield with a mirror finish.
You may attune to or break attunement from this shield in the same action you use to don or doff it. 
While you have the shield donned, you gain the following benefits:
- You have a +2 bonus to Dexterity saving throws.
- When you are targeted by the *magic missile* spell or would be hit by one or more ranged spell attacks, roll a d4. On a 4, the spell is reflected in a direction of your choosing. If the triggering spell is magic missile, choose new targets for each missile that would hit you. If the triggering spell is not magic missile, choose a new target for each triggering spell attack and reroll each attack using a +5 as the ranged spell attack modifier.
