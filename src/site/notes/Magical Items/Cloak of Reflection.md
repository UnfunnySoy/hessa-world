## Overview
## Mechanics
***Wondrous Item, very rare (requires attunement)***
A large azure cloak embroidered with silver thread.
The cloak has 3 charges. When you are hit by one or more ranged spell attacks or targeted by the magic missile spell, you can use your reaction to expend one of the cloak’s charges and cast the redirect spell. When you make the spell’s checks, you do so with a bonus of +7. The cloak regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn.
