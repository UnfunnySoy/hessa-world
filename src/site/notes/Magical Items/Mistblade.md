## Overview
## Mechanics
***Weapon (any sword), uncommon (requires attunement)***
A thin sword with visions of a misty landscape on the face of the blade.
While you are wielding Mistblade, you may cast the *misty step* spell once using a bonus action on your turn. You regain the ability to do so after you complete a short or long rest.
