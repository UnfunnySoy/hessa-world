## Overview
## Mechanics
*3rd level abjuration*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 120 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a vial of water from a mountaintop)
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
In a 20-foot radius, 40-foot high cylinder centered on a point you choose within range, light shines from an illusory silver moon while thick snowflakes slowly flutter to the ground. Until the spell ends, dim light is cast from the moon, and the area inside of the cylinder is lightly obscured by snowfall. When you cast this spell, you may choose any number of creatures you can see to draw strength from this spell.
While in the spell’s area, a chosen creature becomes resistant to cold damage and gains 1d6 temporary hitpoints at the start of each of its turns. Additionally, chosen creatures in the spell’s area have advantage on all ability checks made as a part of the casting of a spell, such as with *counterspell* or *dispel magic*. 
Creatures in this spell’s area other than the ones you have chosen take 2d6 cold damage at the end of each of your turns while in the spell’s area and have their speed halved until they leave the spell’s area.
