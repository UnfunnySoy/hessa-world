## Overview
## Mechanics
*1st level enchantment*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 1 round
You attempt to breach the mind and frazzle the reflexes of a creature that you can see within range. The target must make an Intelligence saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 4d4 psychic damage and cannot take reactions until the end of your next turn. On a success, the target takes half as much damage and can take reactions as normal.
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 1st.
