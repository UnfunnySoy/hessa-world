## Overview
## Mechanics
*1st level evocation*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous
You hurl a bolt of powerful and abrupt magical energy. You choose fire or lightning for what kind of magical burst you create, and then make a ranged spell attack against a creature within range. If the attack hits, that creature takes 3d6 damage of the type you chose. 
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st. Additionally, if you roll a 6 on any damage die for this spell, you regain a spell slot equal to the level of the spell slot used to cast this spell minus one.
