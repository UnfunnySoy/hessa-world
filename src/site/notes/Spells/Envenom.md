## Overview
## Mechanics
*2nd level transmutation*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 30 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a small vial of animal venom)
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
Choose up to 6 slashing or piercing weapons and up to 30 pieces of ammunition within range. The chosen implements become coated in a basic poison for the duration of the spell. A creature hit by a poisoned weapon or piece of ammunition takes an additional 1d6 poison damage. A creature may only take this extra damage once per turn. The spell’s magic ends on a piece of ammunition when it hits or misses a target.
***Spell Lists.*** Wizard, Artificer, Druid, Cleric, Ranger
