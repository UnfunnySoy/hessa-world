## Overview
## Mechanics
*3rd level transmutation*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 120 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a small cube of ice or scarf made of silk)
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
A transparent, slippery film covers the ground in a 20-foot cube originating from a point within range and turns it into difficult terrain for the duration.
When the film appears, each creature standing in the spell’s area must succeed a Dexterity saving throw or fall prone and have its speed reduced to zero until the start of its next turn. A not prone creature that starts its turn in the spell’s area, uses at least half of its movement to stand up in the spell’s area, enters the spell’s area for the first time on its turn, or makes an attack roll in the spell’s area must succeed a Dexterity saving throw or fall prone and have its speed reduced to zero until the start of its next turn.
Additionally, a moving creature or object that enters the spell’s area slides along the film in the direction of its movement until it collides with something or is no longer on the film. If a sliding creature or object collides with a creature or object of the same size or lesser, the two travel 5 feet in the direction of the sliding entity’s movement, then come to a stop. If the sliding entity collides with an entity that is greater in size or a solid wall, then the sliding entity comes to a stop.
