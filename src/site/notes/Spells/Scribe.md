## Overview
## Mechanics
*Transmutation cantrip*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** S, M (1 cp worth of paper and ink which the spell consumes)
**Duration:** Instantaneous
You touch a sheet of paper that contains clear writing and the sheet of paper used in the casting of this spell. Any writing on the initial sheet of paper is then copied to the other sheet, producing an exact copy of the writing on the provided sheet. 
This spell cannot be used to copy spells. 
***Spell Lists.*** Wizard, Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock
