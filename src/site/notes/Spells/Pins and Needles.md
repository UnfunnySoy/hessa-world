## Overview
## Mechanics
*2nd level conjuration*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You surround a humanoid that you can see within range with a cloud of thin ice shards. The target must make a Dexterity saving throw, becoming encircled by icy needles on a failed save. While surrounded by the icy needles, the target takes 1d8 cold damage at the start of each of its turns. If the target willingly moves 5 or more feet, it takes 3d8 cold damage and the spell ends.
The spell ends early for the target if it takes any fire damage.
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional humanoid for each slot level above 2nd.
