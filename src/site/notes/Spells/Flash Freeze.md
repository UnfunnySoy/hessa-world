## Overview
## Mechanics
*2nd level evocation*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 100 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (powdered sea shells and a sprig of mint)
**Duration:** Instantaneous
Protrusions of ice grow from the air around a creature you can see within range. The target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 4d8 cold damage and is restrained by shards of ice for 1 minute or until it or another creature within reach of it uses an action to break the ice. At the start of each of its turns while restrained by the ice, the target takes 1d8 cold damage. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn’t restrained by ice.
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage for the initial freeze increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
***Spell Lists.*** Wizard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Druid, Ranger
