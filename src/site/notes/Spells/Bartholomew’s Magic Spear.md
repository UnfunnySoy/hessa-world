## Overview
## Mechanics
*2nd level conjuration*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self (60-foot line)
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You conjure a glowing spear of magical energy which hovers around your body. For the duration, you may make a special attack using the spear, mystically hurling it towards a point you can see. The spear travels in a line 60 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d6 force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. 
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
