## Overview
## Mechanics
*4th level abjuration*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S, M (a silver cloak worth at least 1,000 gp)
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You touch the cloak used in the casting of this spell. For the duration, the cloak becomes enchanted, granting various benefits to the cloak’s wearer:
- The wearer has advantage on saving throws against spells.
- The wearer may use its action to cast *invisibility* targeting itself at will without expending a spell slot.
- The wearer may use its bonus action to cast *misty step* at will without expending a spell slot.
