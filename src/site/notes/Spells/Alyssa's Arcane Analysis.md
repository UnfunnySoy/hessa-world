## Overview
## Mechanics
*4th level divination*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** S, M (a thin copper coil wrapped around a small amethyst crystal worth at least 50 gp)
**Duration:** Instantaneous
Choose a creature you can see within range. The target must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, you learn 3 of the following pieces of information:
- The target’s Wisdom score.
- The target’s Intelligence score.
- The target’s Charisma score.
- Any and all magical effects affecting the target.
- Any and all spells that the target is concentrating on.
- Any and all magic items the target is attuned to.
- If the target has prepared or is otherwise capable of casting a spell of your choosing.
- The target’s highest available spell slot. If the target has no spell slots remaining, you learn that. 
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you learn one additional piece of information for each slot level above 4th.
