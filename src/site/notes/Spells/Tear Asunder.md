## Overview
## Mechanics
*3rd level evocation*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 30 feet
**Components:** S
**Duration:** Instantaneous
You violently swing your arms, cutting through the air like a knife. Choose a humanoid you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw as sheets of air threaten to tear their body apart. On a failed save, the target takes 7d8 slashing damage. If this damage reduces a target to 0 hit points, the target is torn into large pieces and dies instantly.
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
