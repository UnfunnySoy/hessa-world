## Overview
## Mechanics
*Transmutation cantrip*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You touch a metal object or portion of a metal object no larger than 1 foot in any dimension. The object glows red-hot and becomes workable with the appropriate tools for the duration. Additionally, a creature who comes in contact with the hot metal takes 1d4 fire damage. A creature may only take this damage once per round.
If you target an object held or worn by a hostile creature, that creature must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw to avoid the spell.
The spell’s damage increases by 1d4 when you reach 5th level (2d4), 11th level (3d4), and 17th level (4d4).
***Spell Lists.*** Wizard, Artificer, Sorcerer
