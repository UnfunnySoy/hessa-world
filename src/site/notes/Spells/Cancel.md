## Overview
## Mechanics
*2nd level abjuration*
**Casting Time:** 1 reaction, which you take when you see a creature within 60 feet of you casting a spell
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** S
**Duration:** Instantaneous
You attempt to interrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell. If the creature is casting a spell with a casting time of 1 reaction, the creature’s spell fails and has no effect. Otherwise, the spell takes effect as normal.
This spell cannot be countered.
