## Overview
## Mechanics
*2nd level evocation*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self (30 feet)
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous
You dash forwards while expelling wild sheets of dark flame. Choose fire or necrotic for the type of flame you shoot. Each creature other than you within 5 feet must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d8 damage of the chosen type, or half as much damage on a successful one. Then, you may move up to 30 feet without provoking opportunity attacks. 
If you end this move at least 20 feet away from where you started this move, each creature other than you within 10 feet must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d8 damage of chosen type, or half as much damage on a successful one. 
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
