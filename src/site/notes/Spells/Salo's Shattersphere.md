## Overview
## Mechanics
*5th level abjuration*
**Casting Time:** 1 reaction, which you take when a Large or smaller creature within range is hit by an attack or forced to make a Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution saving throw 
**Range:** 30 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a gold and glass sphere worth at least 100 gp)
**Duration:** Instantaneous
A translucent sphere of force materializes around the target of the triggering attack or saving throw. If the triggering effect is an attack, the attack misses, and the target ignores all damage and other effects of the attack. If the triggering effect is a saving throw, the target automatically succeeds and ignores all damage and effects of the effect which caused the saving throw.
In either case, the sphere then shatters. Each creature other than the target within 10 feet of the sphere must make a Dexterity saving throw as shards of the sphere splinter outwards. On a failed save, a creature takes 3d8 piercing damage, or half as much damage on a successful one.
