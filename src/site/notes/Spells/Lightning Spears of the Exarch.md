## Overview
## Mechanics
*3th level evocation*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self (60-foot line)
**Components:** V, S, M (a hook of twisted copper and gold wire)
**Duration:** Instantaneous
You conjure two crackling bolts of radiant lightning and hurl them forcefully from your grasp. Two strokes of lightning forming lines 60 feet long and 5 feet wide blast outward in directions of your choosing. Each creature in a line must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 2d6 lightning damage and 2d6 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
The lightning ignites flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the lightning damage or the radiant damage (your choice) increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.
