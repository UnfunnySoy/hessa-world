## Overview
## Mechanics
*1st level evocation*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 120 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a small sponge soaked in alcohol)
**Duration:** 1 minute
You evoke a swirling crimson flame and hurl it towards a creature or object within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, a target takes 3d6 fire damage and is engulfed in flame. While engulfed in flame, a target takes 1d6 fire damage at the end of each of its turns. A creature can end this damage by using their action to extinguish the flames.
**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage for the initial hit increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
***Spell Lists.*** Wizard, Artificer, Sorcerer
