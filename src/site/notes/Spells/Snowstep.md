## Overview
## Mechanics
*3rd level conjuration*
**Casting Time:** 1 bonus action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S, M (a small handful of snow)
**Duration:** 1 round
Your form dissipates in a burst of snowflakes, disappearing into the Ethereal Plane (the spell fails and the casting is wasted if you were already on that plane). At the start of your next turn, you return to an unoccupied space of your choice that you can see within 60 feet of the space you vanished from.
You can bring along objects as long as their weight doesn’t exceed what you can carry. If you are outdoors under snowy conditions when you cast this spell, you can bring one willing creature of your size or smaller who is carrying gear up to its carrying capacity with you into the Ethereal Plane. The creature must be within 5 feet of you when you cast this spell, and there must be an unoccupied space within 5 feet of your destination space for the creature to appear in; otherwise, the creature is left behind. The creature then returns to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the destination space at the start of your next turn.
While on the Ethereal Plane, you can see and hear the plane you originated from, which is cast in shades of gray, and you can't see anything more than 60 feet away. You can only affect and be affected by other creatures on the Ethereal Plane. Creatures that aren't there can't perceive you or interact with you, unless they have the ability to do so.
***Spell Lists.*** Druid, Ranger
