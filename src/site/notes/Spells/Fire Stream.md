## Overview
## Mechanics
*Evocation cantrip*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self (15-foot line)
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous
A burst of flame erupts outward in a line 15 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 1d6 fire damage.
The fire ignites flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.
This spell's damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
***Spell Lists.*** Wizard, Artificer, Sorcerer
