## Overview
## Mechanics
*2nd level enchantment*
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous
You force the air out of a being’s lungs in an attempt to suffocate and stifle them. Choose a humanoid that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 3d10 necrotic damage and cannot speak or cast spells with verbal components until the end of your next turn. On a success, a target takes half as much damage and can speak and cast spells as normal.
A creature is immune to this spell if they do not require air to breathe or do not have lungs.
***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 2nd.
